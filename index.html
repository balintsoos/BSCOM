About Me

Hi, I am a 20 years old Front-end developer, JavaScript fanboy and university student from Budapest, Hungary with a passion for making visually stunning and modern web applications. I love beautiful and meaningful content and no matter what I do, I always care about all the little details that make something unique and memorable.

In high school I started to have interest in programming, robotics and space reasearch. As a member of the HungaroBots robotics team we won Google Moonbots 2012 from 147 teams from around the world. In 2014 I started my BSc studies at the Eotvos Lorand University (ELTE) in the Faculty of Informatics as a Computer Scientist and Software Developer.

If you have any further questions about me, my works or if you just want to hang out, please contact me.
